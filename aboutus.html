About Us

  • Welcome to MothersForSale.com! We are a one-of-a-kind service designed to foster meaningful connections between mothers and sons seeking companionship.
  • Founded over two years ago, Mothers For Sale has become a haven for those exploring alternative family dynamics. Although the concept may seem unconventional, our project is very real. Members from all walks of life engage on our platform to find new beginnings through shared experiences, virtual conversations, and in-person meetings. Safety, respect, and integrity are our core values, ensuring that every user feels heard, valued, and respected throughout their journey with us.

Our Journey and Philosophy

  • From the start, we focused on fostering trust and quality interactions. To join, every user must select either a mother or son role—this distinction simplifies the relationship-building process and avoids potential confusion.

    • How We Keep You Safe

    • We take user safety seriously and have implemented several measures to protect your privacy and maintain community standards. For instance:
      • All communications are facilitated through our platform. If you send a letter, we will handle the delivery to the intended recipient to ensure compliance with our standards.
      • Personal contact details like email addresses are kept strictly confidential and are accessible only to our internal team.
      • We do not sell or share your personal data with any third parties. Our priority is to safeguard your information and uphold our commitment to privacy.
      • We maintain a strict code of conduct to ensure respectful behavior. Any violations, particularly involving inappropriate or sexual behavior, result in immediate action, which may include account suspension, permanent removal, or worse.

    How to Get Started

    • Joining is easy! Simply visit our Join page to create your profile. After entering basic information, uploading a profile photo, and submitting a short bio, our team will review your application. While approval may take time, this process ensures every member aligns with our community's values.
    • Early conversations are essential in building rapport and ensuring compatibility. Rest assured, our dedicated support team is available to address any issues you encounter with other members. If you experience any difficulties, you can report them through our mail service or visit us directly at our office: Underneath 13120 Crenshaw Blvd, Gardena, CA 90249

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • For any other questions, please refer to our FAQ page. If you forget your password, simply click the “Forgot Password?” link on the login page and follow the instructions provided. Your security and user experience are always our top priorities.
    • Thank you for being a part of Mothers For Sale! We look forward to helping you find meaningful connections and build new family relationships.
