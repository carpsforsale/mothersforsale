Privacy Statement for MothersForSale.com, Inc.

MothersForSale.com, Inc. has created this privacy statement to demonstrate our firm commitment to protecting your privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for http://www.mothersforsale.com.

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, administer our website, and collect regional demographic information. Our site also utilizes cookies to keep track of your preferences and to personalize your experience at MothersForSale.com. We use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to track your usage history.

Our site's registration form requires visitors to provide us with contact information (such as their name and email address). We use customer contact information from the registration form to send users information about our platform and services. Customer contact information is also used to reach out to visitors when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

Our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect visitors' contact information (like an email address) and financial information (like account or credit card numbers). We use our customers' contact information to communicate with them when necessary. Financial information collected is only used to bill users for our products and services.

We partner with an outside advertising company to display ads on our site. These banner ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our website, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our advertising company, and we do not have access to this information.

Public Information

The personal profiles posted on this site are accessible to its visitors. Members of the site may contact other profiles through our messaging system. Your email address is not made publicly available.

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. We have a secure ordering process and a secure certificate registered with a trusted provider, ensuring that all of your personal information is encrypted during transmission. Our internal network is secured behind a robust firewall, with limited access to outside channels.

Children's Guidelines

MothersForSale.com is a community for adults 18 years of age or older. We do not direct any marketing toward minors and do not allow minors to post profiles on our site. All new profiles are moderated, and any listings from minors can be removed by contacting our Customer Service Department.

Choice/Opt-Out

MothersForSale.com provides users with the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about the visitor.

If you prefer not to be contacted by us, you can remove your email address from our database by visiting your profile settings and selecting the checkbox at the bottom of the page. To remove your active profile from our site, simply visit your profile settings and choose to "hide your profile."
