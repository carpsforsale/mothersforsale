Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is "Mothers For Sale"?
"Mothers For Sale" is a service designed to foster new family connections by matching mothers and sons who are seeking meaningful relationships.
Is this a real dating service or just a parody?
This is indeed a real project with a distinctive concept. While the idea may seem unconventional, we assure it is very real. The intent is to provide a space for those looking for deep connections and alternative family dynamics.
Why can't girls join as daughters?
Currently, the service is tailored specifically to mothers and sons to explore this unique dynamic. Previous attempts to include daughters introduced complexities that compromised the user experience, necessitating the discontinuation of that model. While expanding membership options remains a future consideration, our current operational framework, including legal resources, is optimized to support the mother-son relationship exclusively.
How do I create a profile?
To create a profile, go to the "Join" page, enter your basic information, upload a profile photo, and provide a brief bio describing what you seek in a family connection. After submission, your profile will be carefully reviewed by a member of our team. As we prioritize quality and authenticity, each profile is manually vetted by an actual person to ensure it aligns with our standards and safeguards against spam or fraudulent activity. Please allow some time, as every submission receives personalized attention.
Can I join as both a mother and a son?
No, each user must select a single role—either mother or son—at the time of registration to maintain clear relationship dynamics and streamline the matching process. Offering dual roles would create unnecessary complexity and misalignment in expectations, which could disrupt the overall experience.
Do I need to provide personal details to join?
Yes, you’ll need to provide a valid email address and some basic personal information to set up your profile. We will also need a copy of your ID and birth certificate. This helps ensure authenticity among members.
How long does it take for my profile to be approved?
The approval process may require some time, as each profile is personally reviewed by a member of our team to ensure all submissions meet our standards. While we are a passionate group of twelve, navigating the technical systems behind the platform can present challenges, given our limited expertise with computers. Our focus remains on accuracy and quality over speed, so we appreciate your patience as we work through each submission carefully.
Are there any rules or guidelines for member behavior?
Yes, all members are required to adhere to our platform's code of conduct, which underscores the importance of respect, honesty, and effective communication. Compliance with these guidelines is essential for maintaining a positive environment. Any violations may lead to consequences, including suspension or permanent removal from the platform. Additionally, we take any form of inappropriate behavior, particularly sexual conduct between members, extremely seriously, and such actions may result in more severe repercussions than a ban.
What should I do if I encounter a problematic user?
If you experience inappropriate behavior, you are encouraged to report the user through mail or by visiting our offices directly. Our dedicated support team will carefully assess the complaint and take appropriate action to address the issue. Your feedback is vital in maintaining a safe and respectful community, and we appreciate your proactive involvement in upholding our standards.
Can I chat with members before meeting them in person?
Absolutely! Our platform actively encourages communication through various channels, including direct messaging and phone calls. For those who are comfortable, email correspondence is also an excellent way to connect. Engaging in conversation prior to scheduling in-person meetings fosters better understanding between members. However, it is important to note that all messaging must occur through our service. For any written correspondence, please send it to us, and we will facilitate the delivery to the intended recipient. This policy ensures that all communication aligns with our standards and maintains the integrity of interactions within our community.
I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
To reset your password, please click on the “Forgot Password?” link located on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions provided in the email sent to your inbox to successfully reset your password.
Is my information safe on this site?
Yes, your information is safeguarded using encryption and secure servers, in compliance with our privacy policy. We prioritize data protection; however, please be aware that our team reviews all correspondence and profiles to ensure adherence to our guidelines and maintain the integrity of the platform.
Can other members see my email address or contact details?
No, your contact details are kept strictly confidential. Only our team at the office has access to your personal information.
Do you share my data with third parties?
No, we do not sell or share your personal information with third parties under any circumstances. Your privacy is our utmost priority, and we are committed to safeguarding your data throughout your experience on our platform.
Is there a recommended way to introduce myself to potential matches?
Yes, we encourage members to approach introductions with honesty and kindness. Begin with a brief introduction about yourself, explaining your purpose for joining the platform and outlining the type of family connection you wish to cultivate. This approach fosters a positive atmosphere and helps establish meaningful connections from the outset.
